Pozole

Picture taken from allrecipes.com

Description

Pozole is a traditional Mexican stew made with meat, hominy, chile peppers, and various seasonings. It is usually topped with cabbage, limes, oregeno, onions, avocados, and radishes.

Pozole verde is made with a green sauce, while pozole rojo is made with a red sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings
  • Tomato
  • Guajillo Chile
  • Hominy
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Boil, then simmer pork with garlic and salt until the meat is cooked through.
  2. Boil tomatoes and chiles together. Drain.
  3. Blend tomato and chiles with salt, garlic, oregano, cumin, and water.
  4. Shred pork, then discard bones and garlic.
  5. Stir sauce and pork into the pot, then simmer until flavors have melded.
  6. Ladle pozole into bowls and finish each serving with toppings.
